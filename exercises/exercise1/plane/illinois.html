let's go anywhere

Who knew you would be flying by yourself for the first time to Moline, Illinois? And for a job interview too! It was so weird having someone with a sign waiting for you at the airport when you arrived. They paid for the entire trip--and a hotel suite with a king bed. It was a whirlwind two days, but it led to the most amazing summer in Iowa. You toured the headquarters of John Deere and learned a lot more about tractors than you ever would have thought.
