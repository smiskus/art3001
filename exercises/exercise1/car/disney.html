Your home away from home, Disney World. It's hard to even count how many times you've been here. You grew up as a Disney kid, and it's safe to say that you still are one. You can remember when Hollywood Studios was called MGM Studios. You've explored the tunnels underneath Magic Kingdom, rode on a banshee in Pandora, and tasted food from around the world at the Flower and Garden Festival at EPCOT. Disney has definitely been a big part of your life, and you would love to be a Disney tour guide one day.
