Who We Are

We are adventurers, explorers, travelers, and most importantly readers of fiction! Our book club is over one hundred riders (members) strong. We host monthly meetings to discuss our latest finds, recommend books, and share our lives. We additionally put on special events ranging from socials to volunteering at local schools to read books to children. Books can transport you to new, undiscovered worlds. Are you brave enough to join?
