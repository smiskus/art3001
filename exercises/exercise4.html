Who We Are

We are adventurers, explorers, travelers, and most importantly readers of fiction! Our book club is over one hundred riders (members) strong. We host monthly meetings to discuss our latest finds, recommend books, and share our lives. We additionally put on special events ranging from socials to volunteering at local schools to read books to children. Books can transport you to new, undiscovered worlds. Are you brave enough to join?

Book of the Month

Spotlight Member

Katie Lenz | Member of Three Years

Katie has been part of the paperback riders since August 2017 and has read an astonishing one hundred books this year alone. She has gone above and beyond with her commitment to our community by attending all of our events. When she's not reading, she is messing with breadboards and circuits. One of Katie's favorite books is Court of Mist and Fury by Sarah J Maas.
