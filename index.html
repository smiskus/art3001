About Me

INT. SARA’S DESK - NIGHT
There are string lights hung around the room, creating a warm, cozy space. A cup of half drank tea sits at the corner.

SARA

Hey there! Most of the time, I’m working on homework, but all I really want to do is find the motivation to make more art. I’ve been trying to get back into reading, as I used to read a ton in high school. I’m a computer science major, but sometimes I wonder whether I should have chosen something else. I’m about to graduate soon and be stuck in the real world forever, so I’m trying to make the most of this semester while it lasts.
